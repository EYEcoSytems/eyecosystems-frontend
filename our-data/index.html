The Knowledge Graph

A Knowledge Graph of Blind & Low-Vision Community Resources

Eyecosystems maintains a structured, machine-readable knowledge graph of blind and low-vision (BVI) community organizations, events, and service providers. Every record is verified by a trained human reviewer from the BVI community — not scraped, not AI-inferred.

The dataset covers nonprofits, advocacy groups, peer support networks, assistive technology providers, educational resources, and government service liaisons. Each record includes verified contact information, service categories, geographic scope, and a full audit trail linking back to the human verifier.

Good Faith Evidence

Using This Data as Good Faith Evidence

ADA Title II requires covered entities to demonstrate good faith efforts toward accessibility. Eyecosystems data provides the documented, auditable evidence that entities and their GovTech vendors need to show in transition plans, compliance reports, and DOJ inquiries.

Every record in our knowledge graph includes a verifier ID, timestamp, verification method, and confidence score — creating an unbroken chain of documentation that supports good faith compliance claims. Pair our data with your accessibility audit to produce a complete compliance package.
